The Headline

About a Tenth of a Bricks-and-Mortar Build

By skipping the physical clinic entirely (no lease, no fit-out, no reception, no signage, no on-premise servers and none of the ancillary costs that come with a real building) we launched a fully-compliant telehealth clinic for a fraction of what a traditional one would cost to open.

The point isn't that we went cheap; it's that we went smart. Every regulatory box is ticked, the clinical standard is uncompromised, and the system is already live. We simply refused to pour six figures into a building when a Master PC, the right cloud, and disciplined spending could do the same job for a tenth of the price. The $5k contingency is there because there's always something, but look at the rest of this page, and you can see how far a dollar goes when it's spent with intent.
