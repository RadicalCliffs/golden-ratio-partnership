Why we exist

Named for a proportion found everywhere in nature

The golden ratio — 1.618 — is the quiet maths behind a seashell, a sunflower, a galaxy's arm. We took the name because we believe a clinic, done properly, should feel that way too: balanced, unhurried, and built around the patient rather than the volume of scripts. Initial consults are free. The prescriber is never paid more for seeing more. Compliance isn't a chore we tolerate — it's the whole point. We're a small founding team, and the two nurses we hire now will help shape how this clinic practises medicine for years.

The Two Roles

Two nurses. Two very different days. One shared mission.

Pick the role that sounds like you — the detail changes completely depending on who you are.

The Prescriber

Nurse Practitioner

You're the clinical lead. You'll prescribe medicinal cannabis — flower, oils, gummies and vapes — within your scope of practice, via the TGA's Special Access Scheme (SAS-B), with our software and nursing team handling the paperwork around you so you can focus on the patient in front of you.

What you'll own

  • Lead all clinical decision-making and prescribing of medicinal cannabis
  • Run unhurried telehealth consults — assess, plan, and titrate doses properly
  • Build each patient's treatment plan and exit strategy from day one
  • Be the clinical conscience of the clinic — we want care, not three-minute scripts

What you bring

  • Current AHPRA registration as an endorsed Nurse Practitioner
  • Scope of practice that includes (or that you're ready to extend to) medicinal cannabis
  • Confidence prescribing Schedule 8 medicines with the same care you'd give opioids
  • A genuinely patient-first instinct and comfort with telehealth

Why you'll love it

  • Real clinical autonomy and proper time with patients
  • Zero pressure to over-prescribe — the clinic isn't built on consult fees
  • A founding voice in how the clinic practises medicine
  • Compliant-by-design — you'll never be asked to cut a corner
💬
The First Voice

Registered / Enrolled Nurse

You're the heart of the patient experience. You'll handle the day-to-day inbound calls and run the pre-screen calls — gathering history and confirming each patient is genuinely eligible before they reach the prescriber. You make people feel cared for from the very first hello.

What you'll own

  • Be the warm, human first point of contact on inbound calls
  • Run pre-screen calls: history, eligibility, and validating each candidate
  • Own intake, scheduling, and follow-up coordination
  • Keep patients feeling looked-after between appointments

What you bring

  • Current AHPRA registration as an RN or EN
  • A brilliant phone manner and genuine empathy
  • Organised, reliable, and comfortable with everyday technology
  • Cannabis experience not required — we'll train you fully

Why you'll love it

  • You are the patient relationship — the role genuinely matters
  • Flexible, telehealth-first and work-from-home friendly
  • Grow with the clinic as we scale
  • A supportive, values-led founding team around you
